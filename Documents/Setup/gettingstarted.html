Getting Started

Welcome to MBCIU! This guide will walk you through the basics of getting your analysis environment set up, submitting your first job, and understanding results. Follow along step-by-step or jump to any section below.

Table of Contents

  • 1. Environment Setup
  • 2. Submitting Your First Job
  • 3. Viewing Results
  • 4. Tips and Troubleshooting

1. Environment Setup

Begin by loading the required modules and cloning the repository:

module load python/3.9
 git clone https://github.com/username/project.git

2. Submitting Your First Job

Use a basic SLURM script to submit your first job:

sbatch job_script.sh

3. Viewing Results

After your job completes, check output files:

less slurm-1234.out

4. Tips and Troubleshooting

For common issues, try checking environment variables or reaching out on Slack:
